Brand Story

Inspired by the shell, shaped for the home.

Lapsnail is rooted in the symbolic meaning of the snail: a living creature whose shell represents protection, comfort, and the sacred intimacy of home.

“Home is where the Shell is.”

This idea sits at the heart of Lapsnail: lighting that helps create a warm, beautiful space where people feel comforted and satisfied.

Lapsnail Space

Lapsnail Space is inspired by the snail. Snail has an impressive history as a symbol throughout the world for the sacred space of the home. Snail’s motto: “Home is where the Shell is”. Within this space, snail rules the roost finding comfort and satisfaction.

We hope to create a warm and beautiful space through the lamps, as comfortable and satisfying as a snail's shell is to a snail. To us, lighting is not only practical. It is emotional. It shapes how a place feels, how people gather, and how an outdoor area becomes part of home.

That is why Lapsnail focuses on outdoor solar lighting that is elegant, approachable, and easy to enjoy — helping customers create a softer, more beautiful outdoor lifestyle.
