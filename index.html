Our Story

Inspired by the comfort of a shell.

The Lapsnail brand story begins with a simple idea: home should feel safe, warm, beautiful, and deeply personal. That philosophy guides how we think about light.

“Home is where the Shell is.”

Snail’s motto reflects the comfort, satisfaction, and sacred sense of home that inspired the Lapsnail brand.

Lapsnail Space

Lapsnail Space is inspired by the snail. Across cultures, the snail has long carried symbolic meaning connected to home, shelter, and sacred personal space. Within its shell, the snail finds comfort and satisfaction.

We hope to create a warm and beautiful space through our lamps, as comfortable and satisfying as a snail’s shell is to a snail. For us, lighting is not only about brightness. It is about emotion, atmosphere, and the feeling that a space truly belongs to you.

This is why Lapsnail is dedicated to outdoor solar lighting that feels inviting, elegant, and easy to live with — products that help turn outdoor areas into an extension of home.
